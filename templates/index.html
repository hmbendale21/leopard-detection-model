The Mission

Why LeopardEye?

IIITDM Jabalpur campus borders the Dumna Nature Reserve — home to leopards that occasionally stray into the campus, posing serious danger. LeopardEye provides instant, AI-powered alerts to protect every life on campus.

🎯

Two-Stage AI

YOLO11 first-pass detection followed by MobileNetV3 crop verification eliminates false positives with surgical precision.

⚡

Instant Alerts

Audible beep alerts trigger within milliseconds of confirmed leopard detection, with smart 2-second cooldown to avoid noise.

🔒

Zero False Positives

Custom ImageNet leopard-family class verification (288, 289, 290) ensures only true leopard detections are flagged.

Under the Hood

The Technology

01
Camera Input
Live webcam stream captured at 640×480 via OpenCV. Motion-blur sharpening applied before inference.
→
02
YOLO11 Detection
YOLOv11 nano model scans every frame for animal candidates at 30% confidence threshold across 9 COCO animal classes.
→
03
MobileNetV3 Verify
Each candidate crop classified by MobileNetV3-Small. Only ImageNet leopard classes (288/289/290) pass the 15% threshold.
→
04
Alert + Overlay
Confirmed leopards get EMA-smoothed bounding boxes, confidence score overlay, and an audible beep alert.
